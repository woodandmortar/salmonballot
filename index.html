Finding your bearings





Heading is the direction your nose is pointing. Not necessarily the direction you are travelling in. Bearing is the direction to some target outside of your vehicle, say a destination or a port of origin. The bearing might be absolute (according to a compass) or relative (according to your heading).

Political headings act as broad categories that encompass the ideological leanings and policy priorities held by a significant segment of the population. By analyzing public opinion polls, focus groups, voting patterns, and demographic data, political strategists can identify clusters of voters who share compatibilities across a range of issues. Headings like "conservative," "progressive," or "libertarian" summarize the prevailing direction of political beliefs within these groups. The headings help simplify political dialogue by reducing complex overlapping views down to a few familiar labels.
